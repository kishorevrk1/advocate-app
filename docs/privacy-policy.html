Privacy Policy

Advocate App  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026

Advocate is an AI-powered legal information tool. We take your privacy seriously. Your case details are used only to generate legal information for you and are never sold or used for advertising.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following when you use Advocate:

  • Account information: email address and password (via Supabase Auth)
  • Case data: dispute descriptions, opponent names, amounts, and states/regions you enter
  • Generated documents: demand letters, phone scripts, and analyses stored to your account
  • Usage data: app interactions (via Mixpanel, if enabled)
  • Purchase information: subscription status via RevenueCat (we never see your payment card)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate AI-powered legal information relevant to your dispute
  • Store your cases and documents for later access
  • Improve the app (aggregated, anonymized analytics only)
  • Send important account notifications (no marketing without consent)

3. AI Processing Disclosure

Important: When you submit a case for analysis, your description is sent to a third-party AI service (Groq, Inc. or Anthropic, PBC) to generate legal information. Please do not include highly sensitive personal information (SSNs, bank account numbers, medical records) in case descriptions.

AI providers process your data under their own privacy policies and do not retain data for training purposes under their API terms.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Groq, Inc. / Anthropic, PBC — AI text generation
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchases
  • Mixpanel — anonymous usage analytics

We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Data stored in encrypted Supabase databases (hosted on AWS)
  • All transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Row-level security — you can only access your own cases
  • Data retained while your account is active

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, and delete all data we hold about you.

EU/UK (GDPR): You have the right to data portability, restriction of processing, and to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

California (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale (we do not sell data).

7. Children's Privacy

Advocate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: privacy@advocate-app.com

Advocate is not a law firm. Nothing in this app constitutes legal advice. For complex legal matters, always consult a licensed attorney.
